About Me

Hi! I'm Anusha Choudhary, a creative enthusiast and technical professional based in the Washington DC-Maryland-Virginia (DMV) area. I have been a photographer for 12 years, having previously worked at the UVA Department of Music as a concert and headshot photographer, and self published my photos and poetry in Seeking Sunlight (2017). I believe photography services should be affordable and uniquely tailored to the subject. If you are looking to collaborate with a photographer who captures not just how a defining moment of your life looks, but how it feels, I welcome you to reach out to me via email!
